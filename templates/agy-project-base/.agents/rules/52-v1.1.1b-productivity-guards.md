# Stabilization Guards Carried into v1.2

## Repair loop limits

- `/nextphase`: at most one targeted repair pass after a failed check.
- `/fixcritical`: at most two targeted repair passes.
- `/fastpatch`: no repair loop; a failed final gate requires `/auditphase` or `/nextphase`.

After the limit, mark the state BLOCKED and request review.

## Fastpatch truth

`/fastpatch` completion requires the final diff and a passing `scripts/Test-FastPatchAllowed.ps1 -RequireChanges` result. A clean preflight is not completion evidence.

## Hook truth

Hook scripts are manual guards unless `.agents/hooks.json` is non-empty and verified by a local Antigravity hook probe.

## Evidence truth

Command exit codes, diffs, tests, logs, screenshots and artifact manifests are verification. Model-written evidence is only a pointer.
