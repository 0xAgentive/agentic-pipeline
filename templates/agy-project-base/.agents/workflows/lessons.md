# /lessons

Review a completed or failed phase and extract reusable lessons.

Write candidate lessons only to a project lesson/inbox document or evidence note.

Do not mutate `.agents/rules`, workflows, hooks or templates automatically.

Each candidate must include: trigger, evidence, scope, proposed rule, false-positive risk and whether human approval is required.

Stop after recording candidates.
