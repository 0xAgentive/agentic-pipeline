# /reportqa

Do not implement features.

Check generated PDF/HTML/ZIP/CSV/MD/JSON artifacts for:

- manifest and checksums;
- no forbidden claims;
- no raw enum labels;
- no unredacted local paths;
- no full device IDs;
- no raw logs by default;
- methodology and limitations sections;
- consistency across report formats.

Output pass/fail and evidence paths.
